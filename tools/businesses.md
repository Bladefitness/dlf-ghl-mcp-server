# Businesses & Companies

Business profiles, companies, and contact associations.

## Businesses

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `ghl_list_businesses` | List businesses in a location |
| `ghl_list_businesses_v2` | List businesses (v2 API) |
| `ghl_get_business` | Get a business by ID |
| `ghl_create_business` | Create a business |
| `ghl_update_business` | Update a business |
| `ghl_delete_business` | Delete a business |
| `ghl_get_contacts_by_business` | Get all contacts linked to a business |

## Companies

| Tool | Description |
